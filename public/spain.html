Discover Spain

A Land of Passion, History, and Culture

La Sagrada Familia

La Sagrada Familia in Barcelona is one of the most iconic and unique architectural feats in the world. Designed by Antoni Gaudí, this basilica blends modernist, Gothic, and naturalistic forms.

Alhambra

The Alhambra in Granada is a stunning palace and fortress complex. Its intricate Islamic architecture, lush gardens, and beautiful courtyards make it a must-visit historical site.

Park Güell

Park Güell in Barcelona, also designed by Gaudí, is a whimsical park featuring colorful mosaics, curving architecture, and spectacular views of the city, making it a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Royal Palace of Madrid

The Royal Palace of Madrid is the official residence of the Spanish Royal Family. Its lavish architecture, grand halls, and magnificent gardens reflect Spain’s royal heritage.

Plaza Mayor

Plaza Mayor in Madrid is a grand square surrounded by historic buildings. Its vibrant atmosphere, perfect for dining and people-watching, makes it a central hub in Spain's capital.

Seville Cathedral

Seville Cathedral is the largest Gothic cathedral in the world. It houses stunning art and the tomb of Christopher Columbus, with breathtaking views from its Giralda tower.

Picasso Museum

The Picasso Museum in Barcelona showcases the works of Pablo Picasso. With over 4,000 pieces, the museum is a tribute to the influential artist’s development and creativity.

Guggenheim Museum Bilbao

The Guggenheim Museum in Bilbao is a striking piece of modern architecture. Its curvaceous titanium structure houses impressive contemporary art exhibitions, drawing art lovers from around the world.

Pamplona

Pamplona is famous for the Running of the Bulls during the San Fermín festival. The event attracts visitors worldwide who come for the thrill and the vibrant local celebrations.

Camino de Santiago

The Camino de Santiago is a famous pilgrimage route across northern Spain. Pilgrims walk through picturesque landscapes, quaint villages, and historic towns to reach the shrine of St. James in Santiago de Compostela.
